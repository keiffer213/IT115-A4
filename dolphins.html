Porpoises

Harbor Porpoise

  • Length: Up to 12 ft
  • Weight: Up to 120 lbs
  • Lifespan: Up to 50 years
  • Diet: Krill and small fish
  • Threats: Toxins and debris
  • Distribution: Puget Sound
  • Conservation Status: Protected under the Endangered Species Act and the Marine Mammal Act
  • Sightings in the Pacific Northwest: Puget Sound, Alki Beach, San Juan Islands, British Colombia
